About Mathematics Center

Located in Pretoria, Mathematics Center is run by qualified and experienced mathematics teaching and coaching professional, Ansie van Rooyen. She moderated national exam papers, holds B.A.(Mathematics Psychology), H.E.D.(Mathematics) from the University Pretoria and is registered with the S.A.C.E.
