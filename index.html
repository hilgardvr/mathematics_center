About The Mathematics Centre

Located in Pretoria, Mathematics Centre was started in 2012 to address the shortage of quality math tution. The center is run by qualified and experienced mathematics teaching and coaching professional, Ansie van Rooyen.


She has moderated national exam papers, holds B.A.(Mathematics Psychology), H.E.D.(Mathematics) from the University Pretoria and is registered with the S.A.C.E.


What Our Children Say
